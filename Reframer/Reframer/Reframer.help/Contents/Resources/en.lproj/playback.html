Frame Input

For precise navigation, type the exact frame number:

  1. Click the frame counter in the control bar
  2. Type the frame number
  3. Press Enter to jump to that frame

Use arrow keys while the field is focused:

  • ↑ / ↓ — Step by 1 frame
  • ⇧↑ / ⇧↓ — Step by 10 frames
