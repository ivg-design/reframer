Reframer

A transparent video overlay for macOS

Reframer lets you overlay reference videos on your screen while working in other applications. Perfect for animation reference, motion design, UI/UX work, and learning from video tutorials.

Key Features

  • Transparent overlay — No window chrome, just pure video
  • Always on top — Stays visible above all other windows
  • Frame-accurate navigation — Step through videos frame by frame
  • Zoom and pan — Inspect details at any scale
  • Adjustable opacity — Blend with your workspace
  • Video filters — Apply and chain real-time effects like edge detection and sharpening
  • Lock mode — Click through the video to interact with apps below
